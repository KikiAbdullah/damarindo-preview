Palm Kernel Expeller

Description

Palm Kernel Expeller is one of the by-products produced from processing palm kernel oil into oil. PKE is in the form of a powder like soil. PKE is usually used as feed for livestock in several countries in the world. Call it China, Vietnam, Thailand and New Zealand.

Exporters must be registered in the country of destination and meet administrative and technical requirements

In an effort to increase export acceleration, the Agricultural Quarantine Agency in collaboration with the Technical Implementation Unit (UPT) conducts intensive guidance and guidance in meeting technical requirements. Among them, the storage warehouse must comply with standards (capable of preventing the transmission of pests/OPTKs to the carrier media and able to guarantee that PKE is free from pests/OPTKs in the warehouse until loading onto ships)
