Wood Pellet

Description

Wood Pellet is an alternative fuel made from sawdust, or wood material. Generally, this fuel is used by countries with 4 seasons, as an alternative fuel to replace coal. There are quite a number of benefits that users can experience when using this one raw material, such as it can be used to heat a room, cook on a stove, burner or something else.

Use of Wood Pellets:

  • As fuel for cooking stoves. The technique is not much different from before. Namely as an alternative fuel for heating cooking stoves, such as stoves or others. Generally, this fuel is used by the industrial world, both small and large scale, such as tofu factories, and others.
  • As a generator of electricity. What is meant is as asource of fuel to replace fossil fuels, which arecurrently running low on reserves. In addition, thisone fuel, including natural fuels, comes from therecycling of wood-related industries.

Considering that this one fuel is indeed very much in this country, it has created a wood pellet industry, continues to move into the export market, and has penetrated into several countries such as South Korea, Japan and others.
